The Best Movies According to Jade Mark

My top 3 movies of all time.

The Shawshank Redemption

A powerful story about hope, friendship, and perseverance. Set in a prison, the movie shows how patience and belief can overcome even the darkest situations.

The Godfather

A legendary crime drama that explores family, power, and loyalty within the Italian-American mafia.

The Dark Knight

More than a superhero movie, this film dives into chaos vs justice, highlighted by an unforgettable villain.
