What Is the Image Element?
The image element (<img>) is used to display images on a webpage.

It is a self-closing (void) element, meaning:
    - It does not have a closing tag
    - All information is provided through attributes


Main Purpose
    - Show photos, icons, logos, and graphics
    - Enhance content visually
    - Support branding and user experience
